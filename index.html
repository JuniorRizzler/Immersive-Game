Courier Contract

The lower city is running on emergency power. Level 1: collect 3 cores and return before 1:20. Level 2: clear 2 laps and 6 cores before 2:45. Level 3: clear 3 laps and 9 cores before 3:30.

Turn: Use arrows, touch drag, or Gyro tilt to steer.
Big turns: Hold A/Q or D/E while turning to drift harder.
Overdrive: Drift to charge, then hold Spacebar on straights.
Ghost: A blue trace follows your previous run.
Allow motion access if prompted.
Click/Touch to continue.
